Our Mission

The RMC Community is keeping up to produce "cream of the crop" graduates who are research-driven and community service-oriented, globally competitive, and life-long learners.

Our Vision

The Rizal Memorial Colleges Inc. envision to become one of the leading private educational institutions in the ASEAN region.

Educational Philosophy

Guided by the dream of our national hero, Dr. Jose P. Rizal, RMC deeply advocates a constructivist and progressivist philosophy of education where students holistic development is underscored. The institution believes that students are motivated to share knowledge through scientific inquiry and demonstrate life and industry-ready competencies through outcomes-based pedagogy.
