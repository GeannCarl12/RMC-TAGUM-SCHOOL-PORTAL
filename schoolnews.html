Clout Leadership

by Luis Gabriel Lapuz

When servant leadership turns to clout, the taste of bitterness and pride comes in and out. Organizations have their own goals and self-unique functions and goals, they range from student councils, that represent the community of young leaders, to international organizations that focus on teaching the nation about learning and the importance of staying updated on school events and news.
